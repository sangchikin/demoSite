## Course Overview

This comprehensive course will teach you everything you need to know about stock market investing.

### What You'll Learn

- Understanding stocks and how markets work
- Fundamental vs. Technical analysis
- Building a diversified portfolio
- Risk management strategies
- Common investing mistakes to avoid

### Target Audience

- Beginners with little to no investing experience
- People wanting to build long-term wealth
- Those interested in passive income generation

### Course Structure

1. **Module 1:** Stock Market Basics (Week 1)
2. **Module 2:** Research & Analysis (Week 1-2)
3. **Module 3:** Portfolio Building (Week 2-3)
4. **Module 4:** Risk Management (Week 3-4)

## Who Should Take This Course?

This course is perfect for anyone who wants to start investing but doesn't know where to begin. No prior financial knowledge required!
